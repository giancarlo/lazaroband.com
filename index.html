This is LAZARO

From left to right: Pablo Salazar, 3rd guitarist and elecronic pianist. Arturo Garcia extravagant rhythm guitarist, back up vocalist, and deep intellectual award winning song writer. Kevin Andrade with his crazy unique style on drums. Giancarlo Bellido with his awe dropping lead vocals, songwriter, and slinging zingy solos on lead guitar. Oscar Amaya, the rock hard headbanging bassist!

Throughout the past year Lazaro has performed at events, fundraisers, bars, and bigger venues like The State Theater in Virginia and The Howard Theatre in DC. Lazaro's goals are nowhere near met.
